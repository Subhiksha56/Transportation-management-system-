What is Transportation?

Transportation is the movement of people, animals, and goods from one location to another. It plays a vital role in the functioning of societies and economies, connecting individuals, businesses, and cultures.

Types of Transportation

Land Transport

Land transport involves the movement of people and goods on roads and railways. Cars, buses, and trains are common examples of land transport.

Air Transport

Air transport allows people and goods to travel quickly over long distances. Airplanes and helicopters are common air transport methods.

Water Transport

Water transport refers to the movement of people or goods via waterways such as rivers, lakes, and oceans. Ships and boats are commonly used in water transport.
